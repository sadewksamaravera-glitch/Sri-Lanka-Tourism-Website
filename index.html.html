Highlights of Sri Lanka

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Ancient rock fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site. Climb the Lion Rock for breathtaking views.

Sacred City of Kandy

Home to the Temple of the Tooth and the colorful Esala Perahera festival.

Tea Plantations

Lush green hills of Nuwara Eliya – enjoy a cup of world-famous Ceylon Tea.

Stunning Beaches

From Mirissa to Unawatuna – golden sands, whale watching & perfect sunsets.

Wildlife Safaris

Yala & Udawalawe National Parks – home to elephants, leopards & exotic birds.

Colombo

The vibrant commercial capital blending modern life with colonial charm.
